About Me

When I graduated highschool in the midst of Covid19, I still wasn’t sure which school I wanted to attend. Most campuses were closed at the time, so as I was searching for a school, I was working a full time job as a ranch hand. I knew I had interests in careers that involved problem solving, so the tech and dev job area caught my attention. After doing some research, I decided I wanted to go into the dev industry. A friend recommended DevMountain, and suggested I give it a try. I immediately fell in love with writing code and figuring out how to make it work.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy outdoor activities, including horseback riding/training, archery, hiking, and fishing. I also really enjoy photography, gaming and many different movies/tv shows.
